Community-Powered Arts

Back the band. Boost the drama. Build the show.

We’re a volunteer 501(c)(3) supporting K-12 music and drama across Girard School District — funding instruments and repairs, sets and costumes, travel, scholarships, and the little essentials that make performances feel big.

About the Boosters

What we fund

  • Scholarships, lessons, and uniform/fee assistance
  • Sets, costumes, sound/lighting, pit and tech needs
  • Travel for festivals, camps, and adjudications
  • Signature events: craft fairs, raffles, concessions
